My Brownie Recipe

By Anton

This recipe will show you how to bake the perfect brownie in your kitchen

Step 1

For the perfect brownies you will need the perfect ingredients! Those are listed here:

  1. 140g Butter
  2. 200g Dark Chocolate
  3. 150g Sugar
  4. 2 Eggs
  5. 1 ES Cocain Powder
  6. 100g Weed
  7. 1 CS Vanilla Extract
  8. Salt

Step 2

Prepare a baking pan with baking paper. Turn on the oven on 180° top- and bottom heat.

Step 3

Chop the chocolate into small pieces.

Step 4

Add some butter to the chocolate. Now you can melt the whole thing in the microwave or in hot water.

Step 5

Stir the melted mass together so it gets one mass.

Step 6

In this next step put the eggs, sugar and a little bit of salt all together in one bowl and stir it together. In the end it should be one white mass.

Step 7

Now you can put it together with the choco-butter mixture together in one bowl. You can now stir it together and go on with the next step right afterwards.

Step 8

Add the vanilla extract such as the cocoa powder and the wheat powder to the bowl.

Step 9

Stir all together until its one mixture. Now the dough is finished.

Step 10

Brush the mixture evenly on to the baking pan. Now put it in the oven for 25 minutes.
